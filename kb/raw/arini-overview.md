## What They Do
AI voice receptionist for dental practices. Answers phone calls 24/7, schedules appointments, handles patient inquiries about insurance and billing. Plugs into existing dental practice management software.

## Target Market
Dental clinics and dental groups in the US. Focus on multi-location practices and Dental Service Organizations (DSOs).

## Features
- 24/7 call answering
- Appointment scheduling into existing PMS
- Insurance and billing inquiry handling
- Revenue recovery from missed calls
