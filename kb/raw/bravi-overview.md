# Bravi — AI Operating System for Home Services

Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/bravi, https://bravi.app
Captured: 2026-04-05

## Company
- YC F25 (Fall 2025)
- Founded: 2025
- Location: San Francisco
- Team: 2 founders
- Website: bravi.app

## What They Do
AI operating system for home services businesses. AI voice and chat assistants for inbound communication, lead management, quote generation, and internal workflow automation. Replaces front office staff for home service installers.

## Target Market
- Home services installers: shutters, windows, garage doors, solar, HVAC, carpentry
- Manufacturers selling D2C
- Fenestration companies (initial focus)
- Digital agencies serving home services contractors

## Features
- 24/7 AI voice and chat assistants
- Lead pre-qualification by project type, urgency, location
- Callback booking, task creation, reminders
- Automatic quote generation and delivery
- AI copilot understanding products, pricing, technical docs

## Founders
- Anas Bouassami (CEO): Family in shutter installation, runs Volet Dumont e-commerce as testing ground. Co-founded Snipfeed (acquired 2024). UC Berkeley Master's, EPFL engineering.
- Pierre-Habté Nouvellon (CPTO): Co-founder of Snipfeed. Aerospace engineer. Math Olympiad medalist.

## Key Problem
"Most installers and manufacturers selling directly to consumers lose 30–40% of potential revenue because they miss calls, don't answer emails, and fail to respond to chats in time."

## Differentiators
- Founder-market fit (family installation business)
- Using own business (Volet Dumont) as real-world testing ground
- B2B2C manufacturer partnerships angle
