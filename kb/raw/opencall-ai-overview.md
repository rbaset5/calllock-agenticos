## What They Do
AI voice and texting agents for service-based businesses. Automates phone call answering and appointment booking. Horizontal play (not vertical-specific).

## Target Market (horizontal)
- Healthcare practices
- Hair salons and spas
- Health clubs and fitness centers
- Mechanics and repair shops
- Therapy/mental health practices
- Retail support

## Features
- Automatic call answering with AI receptionists
- Appointment booking
- Real-time API integration with existing business systems
- No-code setup (under one hour)
- HIPAA compliance for healthcare
- No third-party data sharing (including OpenAI)
- Multi-channel: voice and text agents
