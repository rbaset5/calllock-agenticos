# Drillbit — AI Admin for Residential Contractors

Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/drillbit
Captured: 2026-04-05

## Company
- YC S24 (Summer 2024)
- Founded: 2023
- Location: Austin, TX
- Team: 10 people
- Website: drillbit.com

## What They Do
AI-powered platform automating administrative workflows for residential contractors. AI receptionists + LLM-based quoting + CRM + project management + payments. Full job lifecycle from lead gen through payment collection.

## Target Market
Residential service contractors: plumbers, electricians, house cleaners, lawn care, landscapers, tilers, roofers.

## Features
- AI Receptionists: phone coordination with businesses and homeowners throughout job lifecycle
- AI Estimators: LLM-generated quotes
- CRM system for job updates
- Project management
- Payment processing/collection
- Hands-off operations: contractors focus on field work, AI handles the office

## Founder
Alexander Seutin — Stanford CS & AI. Prior: SpaceX, Apple, Rivian, Built Robotics. Hands-on construction experience renovating homes and Airstreams.

## Market Data
- U.S. home services market: ~$675-700B annually
- Half the cost of typical home service jobs = administrative coordination (not labor/materials)
- Existing CRM solutions have high churn because users must actively manage the platform

## Differentiators
- Phone-based interaction (meets contractors where they are vs forcing them into apps)
- Full lifecycle (lead → quote → project → payment) not just call answering
- "Unlike competitors requiring contractors to change behavior and babysit software"
