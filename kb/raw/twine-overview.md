# Twine — AI Text Message Receptionist for SMBs

Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/twine
Captured: 2026-04-05

## Company
- YC S23
- Founded: ~2023
- Status: Active

## What They Do
24/7 AI receptionist that texts back when businesses miss calls. Not voice AI — text-based. Answers FAQs, books appointments via SMS conversation.

## How It Works
1. Business misses a call
2. Twine texts the customer automatically
3. AI understands natural language, asks what they need
4. Books services, answers questions, follows up

## Target Market
Small businesses broadly (horizontal). "Makes sure small businesses never miss out on customers because of missed calls."

## Founders
- Anand Valavalkar: Started a networking & A/V company in high school (grew to six figures). Sold phone systems to SMBs — saw firsthand the revenue left on the table from missed calls.
- Divyesh K.
- Omar Imtiaz

## Differentiators
- TEXT-based, not voice — different modality entirely
- Responds to missed calls specifically (not replacing the phone)
- Minimal setup — integrates with existing VOIP and cell
- Founder-market fit (sold phone systems, saw the problem)

## Key Insight
"Customers often work with the company that responds to them the fastest." Speed of response > quality of response for SMB lead capture.
