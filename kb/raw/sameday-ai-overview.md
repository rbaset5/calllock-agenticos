# Sameday AI — AI Workforce for the Trades

Source: https://www.gosameday.com/, https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/sameday
Captured: 2026-04-05

## What They Do

AI-powered phone answering and sales system for home service businesses. Not just a receptionist — an AI sales agent that closes jobs on the phone, dispatches, upsells, and follows up.

## Y Combinator

- YC W23 batch
- Positioned as "the leading AI workforce for the trades"

## Target Market

Home service businesses:
- HVAC
- Plumbing
- Roofing
- Solar installation
- Pest control
- Commercial cleaning
- Window cleaning

From single-location to multi-location enterprises.

## Core Product — Multiple AI Agents

### AI Receptionist
- Answers phones 24/7, books appointments
- "Most people can start taking calls in just 15 minutes"

### AI CSR (Customer Service Rep)
- Handles billing, rescheduling, follow-ups, reminders

### AI Sales Agent
- Sells memberships, negotiates payment plans, upsells packages
- Overcomes objections
- "Closes 80% of all sales calls it receives"

### AI Dispatcher
- Prioritizes high-value jobs
- Manages capacity
- Assigns tags

## Voice AI Technology

- "Ultra-human" voice called "Pro Voice"
- Detects customer tone and hesitation to know when to transfer to a human
- Handles multi-step conversations including objection handling
- Stress-tested with "hundreds of thousands of simultaneous phone calls"
- Four redundancy layers

## Performance Metrics

- 92% booking rate across most channels
- 2M+ calls answered
- $1.25B revenue sold by AI agents
- 96% customer satisfaction
- One customer: went from 58 to 208 after-hours bookings

## Integrations

Official partners:
- **ServiceTitan** — capacity management, dispatch, job prioritization (primary integration)
- **FieldRoutes** — subscriptions, upsells, signatures
- **Service Fusion** — scheduling, job board
- **Jobber** — recurring jobs, upsells
- **Housecall Pro** — lead qualification, dispatching
- Also: Zapier, HubSpot, custom APIs, webhooks

Note: Early reports suggested "you need ServiceTitan" but they've since expanded to other platforms.
