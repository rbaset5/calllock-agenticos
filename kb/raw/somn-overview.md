Source: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/somn
Captured: 2026-04-05

## Company
- YC W24
- Founded: ~2023
- Location: San Francisco
- Status: Active

## What They Do
Human-like AI receptionists for healthcare clinics. Answer calls, book appointments, call patients. Handle any phone call involving repetitive tasks.

## Target Market
Healthcare clinics (general, not just dental).

## Features
- AI phone call automation
- Appointment booking
- Patient outreach (outbound calls to patients)
- Integrates with fax systems, EHRs, scheduling software
- No rip-and-replace — works with existing tech
