# ICP — SMB Home Services Contractors

The single source of truth for who CallLock sells to. Use this to qualify leads, filter lists, and write cold-call openers.

## The One-Liner

US-based home services contractors (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage door, drain, water treatment) with 1–5 trucks, $300K–$2M revenue, owner-operator as the buyer, receiving 10+ inbound calls per week with $300+ average service ticket OR $3K+ install ticket, currently missing 20%+ of calls (after-hours, on-the-job, lunch), using QuickBooks/Jobber/Housecall Pro or no system, where the owner or owner's spouse is currently the receptionist and the business has either grown past their capacity or is bleeding leads to faster-responding competitors.

## Hard Filters

Use these to include/exclude a prospect in one pass.

| Dimension | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Geography** | United States |
| **Trades** | HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage door, drain, water treatment |
| **Size** | 1–5 trucks |
| **Revenue** | $300K–$2M/year |
| **Buyer** | Owner-operator (not GM, not office manager) |
| **Call volume** | 10+ inbound calls/week |
| **Ticket size** | $300+ service OR $3K+ install |
| **Miss rate** | 20%+ calls missed (after-hours, on-job, lunch) |
| **Stack** | QuickBooks, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or no system |
| **Receptionist** | Owner or owner's spouse currently answering |

## Qualifying Signals

Evidence the contractor fits — the more that apply, the hotter the lead.

- Owner or spouse is the one answering the phone between jobs
- Business has grown past their capacity to handle inbound
- Already buying leads from Google LSA / paid search ($500+/month)
- Competitors are out-responding them on speed-to-lead
- Explicit complaints about "missing calls" or "voicemail"
- Not locked into ServiceTitan (which has native AI Voice)

## Disqualifiers

Skip these — wrong fit, wrong time, or wrong buyer.

- 6+ trucks or $2M+ revenue → has in-house dispatch or on ServiceTitan
- Pure new construction / commercial-only (no inbound residential calls)
- Answering service already in place and happy with it
- Non-owner buyer (office manager, GM) → different sales motion
- Locked into ServiceTitan AI Voice

## The Two Filters That Carry Positioning

From [[positioning/positioning-dunford]]:

1. **Owner is currently the receptionist** (pain)
2. **Already buying leads** (they know what a lead costs)

If both are true, the missed-call revenue frame sells itself.
