# Patientdesk.ai

AI voice receptionist built specifically for dental practices. YC W26 batch, $1M raised.

## What They Do

24/7 AI receptionist that handles inbound calls, books appointments directly into practice management systems, verifies insurance in real-time, and collects payments. Also runs Meta Ads lead gen campaigns for dental practices.

## Why This Matters for CallLock

Patientdesk validates the vertical AI voice receptionist model — the exact pattern CallLock is pursuing for home services. Key parallels:

- **Vertical-specific integrations:** They integrate with 15+ dental PMS systems (CorePractice, Dentrix, OpenDental). CallLock needs equivalent depth with home services tools (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber).
- **Beyond answering calls:** They don't just answer — they verify insurance, collect payments, and generate leads. This is the "full workflow" play, not just a voice bot.
- **Pricing model:** Monthly subscription + per-minute overage ($0.20/min). Base includes 1,500 minutes. Multi-location discount at 50% of base. This is a reference pricing model for CallLock.
- **ROI framing:** "Pays for itself with just one implant case per month." CallLock needs an equivalent hook — "pays for itself with one booked HVAC install."

## Competitive Intelligence

**Strengths:**
- YC backing gives credibility and network
- Deep vertical focus (dental only) means they can build insurance verification, PMS integrations that horizontal players can't match
- Lead gen pipeline (Meta Ads) makes them a revenue driver, not just a cost saver
- International expansion already (US, Canada, UK, Australia, UAE)

**Weaknesses / Opportunities for CallLock:**
- Dental-only — home services is wide open
- $0.20/min overage could get expensive for high-volume practices
- No mention of outbound calling capability (callbacks, follow-ups)
- Their lead gen is Meta Ads, not signal-based outbound like CallLock's planned Scout pipeline

**Pricing benchmark:**
- 1,500 min/month base (that's ~25 hours of call time)
- $0.20/min overage
- 50% discount per additional location
- No setup fees

## Key Takeaways

1. The vertical AI receptionist model is YC-validated and funded
2. Deep industry integrations (PMS, insurance, payments) are the moat, not the voice AI itself
3. "Revenue driver" positioning (lead gen + booking) beats "cost saver" positioning (replace receptionist)
4. Home services is the same play for a different vertical — and nobody has won it yet
