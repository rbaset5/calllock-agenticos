# Product

Canon on what CallLock *is* for the SMB ICP — features, offering, pitch. Unlike `competitors/` (external research) or `positioning/` (category framing), this dossier is the source of truth for what we actually ship and sell.

## Articles

- [[product/smb-feature-set]] — SMB feature set ranked by deal-closing power: the Core 5, the Next 3 differentiators, the 2 demo winners, explicit non-goals, and the one-sentence offer.

## Conventions

- Feature lists are **ranked**, not alphabetized. Ranking is the content.
- Non-goals ("what to explicitly NOT include") are first-class — they protect the offer from feature creep.
- Every article should tie back to the one-sentence pitch in [[product/smb-feature-set]].
