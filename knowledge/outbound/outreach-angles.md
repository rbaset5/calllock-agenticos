# Outreach Angles

- `after_hours_behavior` -> Lead with the concrete missed-call proof from the probe.
- `paid_demand` -> Lead with wasted paid demand and leakage after business hours.
- `no_backup_intake` -> Lead with the risk of calls dying when the owner is busy.
- `hours_mismatch` -> Lead with emergency-trade expectations versus limited live coverage.
- `owner_operated` -> Lead with the owner's bandwidth problem.
- `review_pain` -> Lead with service reputation damage caused by missed calls.
