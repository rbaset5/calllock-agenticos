# Call Hooks

- `after_hours + no_answer`: "I called your shop at 8pm Thursday and nobody picked up. That's the problem I solve."
- `paid_demand + voicemail`: "You're running Google Ads but calls after 6pm still go to voicemail."
- `owner_operated`: "You're the one answering every call. What happens when you're on a job?"
- `review_pain`: "Missed calls turn into bad reviews faster than most shops realize."
- `no_backup_intake`: "You already have demand. The leak is what happens when no one can grab the phone."
