# Objection Handling

- `We use ServiceTitan.` -> "ServiceTitan routes calls. It doesn't answer them at 2am when nobody is available."
- `We're too small.` -> "That's exactly who this is for. Small shops feel missed calls immediately."
- `How much?` -> "Depends on call volume and coverage window. The faster question is how many paid calls you're losing now."
- `We already have voicemail.` -> "Voicemail captures excuses. Live pickup captures revenue."
- `Call me later.` -> "Fair. Give me the best day and I'll call with the exact leak I found."
