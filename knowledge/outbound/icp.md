# ICP

CallLock's outbound ICP is the small home-service advertiser that already pays for local demand but still handles phones like an owner-operated shop.

- Trades: HVAC and plumbing.
- Company shape: small local operator, often owner-led, usually no true admin layer after hours.
- Demand profile: active Google Ads, Local Services Ads, or branded search demand already exists.
- Pain pattern: after-hours calls ring out, roll to voicemail, or get handled inconsistently.
- Operational gap: no backup intake, no overflow coverage, no reliable handoff when the owner is on a job.
- Best-fit motion: founder-led cold call using a sharp proof point from a recent phone probe.
