## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version is a self-healing product that detects quality issues, diagnoses root causes, and automatically generates fix PRs — all while the founder sleeps. The guardian doesn't just watch; it acts. Combined with visual regression testing and CI contract validation, the product quality bar is higher than most companies with 10x the headcount.
